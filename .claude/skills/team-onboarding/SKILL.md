# Team Onboarding Plan

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need to:
- Build a 30/60/90-day onboarding plan for a new hire
- Design department-specific orientation checklists
- Create buddy/mentor programs for new employees
- Set clear 90-day success milestones

---

## Core Principle

THE FIRST 90 DAYS PREDICT THE NEXT 3 YEARS — A NEW HIRE WHO FEELS LOST IN WEEK 1 STARTS LOOKING FOR OTHER JOBS BY MONTH 3.

---

## Phase 1: Brief

| Input | What to Ask | Default |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **Role** | What position is the new hire joining? | As defined |
| **Start date** | When do they start? | As scheduled |
| **Buddy** | Who will be their go-to person for questions? | Team lead |
| **Tools access** | What systems do they need on Day 1? | Company systems |
| **First project** | What's a good early win for them? | Defined by manager |

---

## Phase 2: 30/60/90 Plan Template

```
## 30/60/90-Day Onboarding — [Name] — [Role]

### Day 1 Checklist (IT & Admin)
- [ ] Workstation and accounts set up
- [ ] Meet the team (intro round)
- [ ] Company overview from CEO/founder
- [ ] Buddy assigned and first meeting scheduled
- [ ] Emergency contacts and HR paperwork

### Week 1 Goals
- Understand company mission, values, and strategy
- Learn team structure and each person's role
- Shadow key meetings and workflows
- Set up all required tools and access

---

### 30-Day Milestone: Learn the Landscape
**Goal:** Understand the business, team, and own role deeply

Learning tasks:
- [ ] Read company strategy docs, OKRs, and previous quarter results
- [ ] 1:1 meetings with 5+ cross-functional colleagues
- [ ] Review existing processes, tools, and documentation
- [ ] Complete required training (product, safety, compliance)

Success criteria:
- Can explain what the company does and how this role contributes
- Knows who to go to for different questions
- Identifies 1 quick win opportunity

---

### 60-Day Milestone: Start Contributing
**Goal:** Deliver early wins and own first responsibilities

Tasks:
- [ ] Complete first independent project or deliverable
- [ ] Propose 1 improvement to existing process
- [ ] Take ownership of defined KPIs
- [ ] Regular 1:1 cadence with manager established

Success criteria:
- First deliverable completed on time and to standard
- Manager feedback: "On track"
- Comfortable operating independently day-to-day

---

### 90-Day Milestone: Full Productivity
**Goal:** Operating fully in the role, building relationships

Tasks:
- [ ] First performance check-in with manager
- [ ] Contribute to team planning or strategy discussion
- [ ] Mentoring or supporting newer/junior team members
- [ ] Review and update own onboarding docs (pay it forward)

Success criteria:
- KPIs trending toward targets
- Peer feedback: positive collaboration
- Clear development goals set for next 6 months
```

---

## Anti-Patterns
- No structured first week — new hire spends Day 1 waiting for laptop setup
- Information dumping without context or prioritization
- No buddy or go-to person identified before start date
- 90 days pass with no check-in on how onboarding is going
