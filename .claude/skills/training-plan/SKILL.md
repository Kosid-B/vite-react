# Training & Development Plan

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need to:
- Build role-specific training curricula from skill gaps
- Design blended learning programs (online, workshop, on-the-job)
- Create a 30/60/90-day onboarding training schedule
- Measure training effectiveness and transfer to job performance

---

## Core Principle

SKILLS ONLY MATTER IF THEY CHANGE BEHAVIOR ON THE JOB — TRACK KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER, NOT JUST ATTENDANCE.

---

## Phase 1: Needs Analysis

| Input | What to Ask | Default |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **Target role** | Which role or team needs training? | All roles |
| **Skill gaps** | From performance review or competency assessment? | From review data |
| **Budget** | Training budget per person per year? | ฿5,000–฿15,000 |
| **Format preference** | Online self-paced, live workshop, or OJT? | Blended |
| **Timeline** | When should skills be operational? | 90 days |

---

## Phase 2: Training Plan Template

```
## Training Plan — [Role] — [Year/Quarter]

### Skill Gap Summary
| Skill | Current Level | Target Level | Priority |
|-------|--------------|--------------|----------|
| [Skill 1] | Novice | Proficient | High |

### Learning Path
#### Month 1: Foundation
- Week 1–2: [Core concept training] — Format: Online · Duration: 4h
- Week 3–4: [Tool/system training] — Format: Workshop · Duration: 8h

#### Month 2: Application
- Practice project: [Real work assignment applying new skill]
- Peer coaching: [Pairing with experienced colleague]

#### Month 3: Mastery
- Assessment: [Practical test or portfolio review]
- KPI check: [Measure behavior change in job performance]

### Success Metrics
- Completion rate: Target 100%
- Assessment pass rate: Target 80%+
- 90-day behavior change score: Target +1 competency level
```

---

## Phase 3: Delivery Methods

| Method | Best For | Cost | Time |
|--------|----------|------|------|
| E-learning | Foundational knowledge | Low | Flexible |
| Live workshop | Skill practice, team alignment | Medium | Scheduled |
| On-the-job | Application and mastery | Low | Ongoing |
| Mentoring | Leadership and judgment | Low | Ongoing |
| External course | Specialized expertise | High | Scheduled |

---

## Anti-Patterns
- Training without a clear skill gap or business need
- One-off workshops with no reinforcement
- No measurement of behavior change after training
- Training calendar built around availability, not priorities
